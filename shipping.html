shipping policy
We endeavour to ship your order within 1-2 business days.

These days, courier times can be a little unpredictable in NZ - but on average, North Island orders will take 2-3 business days to be delivered, and South Island orders will take 1-2 business days. For rural delivery please allow 1-2 days longer.

Shipping within NZ is a flat rate of $8.00.

If you live in the Dunedin area (including Mosgiel) we can drop your order off for free!

Free NZ shipping applies to all orders over $100.

Unfortunately, at this time, we are unable to offer international shipping.
