macrame dolls
As a mother of two, I know firsthand the joy and wonder that comes with welcoming a new life into the world. When my baby girl was born, I was inspired to create something special for her - something that would capture the magic of childhood and bring a smile to her face. That's how my macrame craft business was born.

At Bandhan, we specialize in handcrafted macrame dolls, nursery decor, and baby toys that are not only beautiful but also safe and durable for children to play with. Our products are inspired by the wonder and innocence of childhood, and our mission is to create a world where every child can have a cherished, one-of-a-kind toy to call their own.
